✉️ Writing a difficult email

45 minutes of staring at a blank screen, three drafts, still worried it sounds rude. Maybe you just don't send it.
3 minutes: describe what you want to say and how you want it to feel. Read the draft, tweak two words, send. Done — and it sounds like the best version of you.

📄 Updating a resume

A whole weekend fighting with formatting, unsure what employers want to read, ends with something that feels outdated.
One evening: tell AI your real work history in your own words. It structures, phrases, and formats — you fact-check and approve. Modern, honest, and finished.

🔎 Researching a big purchase

Hours across a dozen confusing websites and reviews that contradict each other. Decision fatigue wins.
10 minutes: ask for a comparison that fits YOUR needs and budget, then ask follow-up questions like you would a knowledgeable friend.
